Some of My Projects

Neural Bots - Unity Game

In 2017, my friend and I participated in a game jam hosted by the well-known game development community YouTuber TairaGames. We had 7 days to create a game from scratch that would fit the theme "Rage Game," inspired by "Getting Over It with Bennett Foddy."

We won the game jam and received a shoutout from TairaGames himself. The game was published on itch.io and received many downloads, but the story didn't end there. A couple of days later, the game was featured on Markiplier's channel and received even more downloads. This was crazy for us; we were just two young developers from Georgia, and our game was played by one of the biggest YouTubers in the world.

This small success inspired me to continue working on the game. I added more mechanics, levels, and even a level editor and authentication system. However, I had to stop working on the game because of my studies and other projects.

I still hope to revisit the project sometime in the future, but with a lower scope to make it more manageable.

Unfortunately, the only build that exists of the game is the original game jam build.

Chess Destroyer - Unity Game

I created this game in 2020. It is a chess game with a unique twist.

Chess Destroyer is similar to a traditional chess game but with additional features: the map expands with each turn, more enemies appear, you can capture an enemy unit by defeating it with your pawn, or acquire new units by completing simple quests that appear in the bottom left corner of the screen.

Defeat enemy units, find allies, protect the king, and survive as long as you can!

Lore: To escape from death, the king had to flee the battleground. With his two pawns, he jumped from his chessboard onto another, smaller one. But it's growing, and enemies are coming.

Mody - React Native App (iOS & Android)

At some point, I picked up React Native and started working with clients to create mobile apps. One of the apps I worked on is Mody, a taxi app for Georgia.

I worked on both the front-end and back-end of the app. I used Firebase for the back-end and Node.js for the server. The app also has a payment system integrated with TBC Bank's API.

Unfortunately, the app didn't gain much traction, but I learned a lot from the experience.

I also worked on several other apps, but this was the most interesting one.
